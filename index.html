FEATURES OF BOOKLOCKER

THE NAME OF THIS SECTION IS CART.
This is the section responsible for the add to cart feature. When a user clicks on the add to cart feature, before they are able to make any payments on the item, they would be redirected here to properly confirm their purchase before being redirected to the payment validation page.

THE NAME OF THIS SECTION IS GENRE.
The video above shows the GENRE feature on the website. when or if a user clicks on the genre button on the nav bar, they are navigated to the GENRE section of the webpage. When user hovers on the GENRE button, a drop down button is displayed showcasing different genres of books for user to choose from. when they choose, user is redirected to a webpage with the books related to that genre they chose.

THE NAME OF THE FEATURE IS "SEARCH".
This section showcases the search feature we were able to implement. When a user types in a keyword in the searchbar, they are able to get some related search items and if they click on what they want on the dropdown, they are able to get details on what they searched for

About BOOKLOCKER

The project "Booklocker" was inspired by a personal love for reading and the need to create an efficient and user-friendly platform for people who are enthusiastic about reading. The idea was born when we thought of something we both have in common which is reading, we thought to create a platform that would solve the challenge of finding books online. If we created a platform that we could access easily and other readers like us without having to go through the stress of unnecessary ads, popups, and redirections. Booklocker is intended to act as a platform for users to easily discover and access books of their choice at will.

The journey began two weeks ago and although it is not 100 percent perfect, we are proud of what we have managed to make out of the time we worked on it. So after weeks of hard work and research, we are proud to present BookLocker. This project is part of our portfolio for the completion of ALX SE FOUNDATIONS.
